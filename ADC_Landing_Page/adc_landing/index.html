The Problem

Every year, the Auditor-General flags the same ICT failures

Government departments spend billions on applications with no structured process to evaluate their value, cost, or risk. The tools don't exist — until now.

📊
Spreadsheet chaos
Application inventories live in disconnected Excel files that are out of date the moment they're saved, with no collaboration or workflow tracking.
💸
Untracked spending
Departments cannot account for total cost of ownership across their portfolio. Licence, maintenance, and implementation costs are siloed across multiple budget lines.
🔍
Audit exposure
Without a formal due diligence process and documented evidence, ICT portfolio decisions cannot withstand AG scrutiny under PFMA and MFMA requirements.

The Application Canvas

Map every application across 9 configurable business domains using a Business Model Canvas-style grid. Rate each against up to 8 custom criteria using a 1–5 star system.

  • ✓
    9 default business domains
    Finance, HR, Operations, IT, Compliance and more — fully renameable and configurable
  • ✓
    1–5 star rating per criterion
    Business Fit, Technical Health, Cost Efficiency, and Risk Level — weights configurable
  • ✓
    Real-time portfolio score
    Weighted average across all rated applications displayed in the header stats bar
  • ✓
    Green / Amber / Red status badges
    Instant visual classification — Adequate (3.5+), Review (2.0–3.4), Critical (below 2.0)

How It Works

From zero to boardroom-ready
in five steps

1
Subscribe and configure
Choose your plan, subscribe online, and receive instant access. Set your organisation name, logo, and configure your 9 business domain labels to match your department's structure.
⏱ 15 minutes
2
Load your application inventory
Add each application to its business domain — name, vendor, and notes. Import from your existing spreadsheet or add manually. No technical skills required.
⏱ 1–2 hours
3
Rate and price each application
Score every application against 4 criteria (1–5 stars). Capture TCO — licence, maintenance, and implementation costs. Assign a Keep / Upgrade / Replace / Retire recommendation.
⏱ 2–4 hours
4
Manage queries and issues
Log any queries that arise during the review — vendor queries, technical issues, contract questions. Track each through the 5-step workflow with a full response thread.
⏱ Ongoing
5
Generate your report
Export a professional PDF or Excel portfolio report. Use the AI Analysis feature to generate a written narrative with prioritised recommendations. Submit to management or the AG.
⏱ 30 minutes
